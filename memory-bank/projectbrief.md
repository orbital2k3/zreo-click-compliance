# Zero Click Compliance (ZCC)

**Autonomous SOC 2 Vendor Risk Management**

ZCC is an AI-powered platform designed to eliminate the manual burden of SOC 2 report reviews. It automates the entire vendor risk lifecycle through an agentic workflow:

1.  **Autonomous Outreach**: Agents contact vendors to request reports.
2.  **AI-Powered Parsing**: OCR and LLM analysis of 150+ page PDFs.
3.  **Exception & Risk Scoring**: Automatic identification of failed controls and risk grading.
4.  **Real-Time Dashboarding**: Premium management interface for security professionals.
