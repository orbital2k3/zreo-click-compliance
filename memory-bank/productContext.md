# Product Context

**The Problem**: Security professionals manually download and read massive PDF SOC 2 reports from hundreds of vendors. This process is time-consuming, prone to error, and hard to track.

**The Solution**: An agent-first platform where you enter a vendor email, and the system autonomously handles outreach, data extraction, and risk assessment WITHOUT user intervention (Zero Click).

**Target Users**:
- CISO / Security Teams
- Vendor Risk Management (VRM) professionals
- Compliance Officers in highly regulated sectors (Finance, Insurance)

**Key Differentiator**:
- "Autonomous Outreach": Unlike other tools, ZCC contacts the vendor on its own.
- "Parser Cluster": Real-time monitoring of the parsing engines.
